## 1. HOLIDAYS & CULTURAL EVENTS IN JULY 2026 NIGERIA

### Islamic Events
- **Muharram (Islamic New Year 1448 AH)**: June 16 – July 14, 2026
  - 1st Muharram falls on Tuesday, June 16, 2026
  - **Key insight:** Early July still carries Muharram significance; avoid flashy promotional content during first week.
- **No major Eid holidays fall directly in July 2026**

### National Holidays
- **No public holidays in July 2026** — Nigeria's national holidays are concentrated in other months

### Cultural Hooks
- **Pre-school resumption shopping peak**: July is prime month for school clothing promotion
- **Back-to-school season marketing window**: July is the last full month before school resumption rush

## 2. LAGOS/NIGERIAN WEATHER IN JULY

- **Rainy season in full effect** (April to October)
- **July is one of the wettest months** in Lagos
- **Typical conditions:**
  - Temperature range: 24°C – 28°C (75°F – 82°F)
  - Humidity: 80-90% (very high)
  - Frequent afternoon/evening thunderstorms

### Implications for Kids Clothing
- **Rain-resistant items** are top sellers: rain boots, waterproof jackets, covered shoes
- **Light layering pieces** work well (thin cardigans, light jackets for air-conditioned classrooms)
- **Dark colors/pastels** are practical for rainy season (less visible stains)
- **School shoes** (closed toes, leather) are in high demand

## 3. WHAT LAGOS PARENTS THINK ABOUT IN JULY – KIDS CLOTHING

### Primary Concerns
1. **School uniforms** – most critical purchase
2. **Back-to-school clothing budget** – many parents spread purchases across July
   - **Thrift appeal**: Quality UK second-hand clothes at 40-70% below retail
3. **Growth allowance** – buying slightly larger sizes to last a full session
4. **Replacement shopping** – after long holiday, kids have outgrown clothes

### What Moms Look For on Instagram (July Pattern)
- **"School warden" aesthetic**: Neat, modest, comfortable
- **Value for money**: "Affordable", "budget-friendly", "delz" (Nigerian slang for deals)
- **UK brand names**: Marks & Spencer, Next, Primark, George (Asda), Tesco F&F
- **Delivery**: "Lagos delivery", "nationwide delivery"
