## 1. KEY CULTURAL ANGLES FOR JULY 2026

### Angle 1: "Rainy Season Glow-Up"
The rainy season (June–September) brings 75–90% humidity across Ogun State and Delta State. Messaging: lightweight, non-comedogenic products that work WITH the weather, not against it.
**Products:** Simple Refreshing Face Wash + Face facts Ceramide Serum

### Angle 2: "New Month, New Skin"
July 1 represents a psychological "second new year" for many Nigerians. Fresh-start energy + new month motivation.
**Products:** All products tied to transformation narrative

### Angle 3: "Back-to-School Glow"
Late July — parents preparing children for school, students wanting a glow-up before new term.
**Products:** Face facts Serum + Nivea Face Cream bundle
